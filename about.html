Who are we

PG Industries (Zimbabwe) Limited (PG Industries or PGIZ) started its operations in the country in Bulawayo in 1948. It began as a merchandiser of glass, timber and hardware products. It was listed on the Zimbabwe Stock Exchange (then Rhodesia Stock Exchange) in 1969.

Over the years it has diversified into a wide range of products and services to include timber, boards, hardware, plumbing, glass, cement products and value added products. PG Industries subsidiaries include: PG Centre, PG Timbers, PG Glass, PG Steel, PG Sawmill, PG Paint and Zimtile. Being in the construction sector PG has grown to be a key player on the national economic development. PG competencies lies in the processing and distribution of building materials from the ground up (start to finish). With over 70 years of experience, PG Industries has erected trusses and tiles in commercial and residential properties in Zimbabwe and the region and these have remained standing for decades and hence the motto

“TRUSTED FOR GENERATIONS”.


01. Vision

To be the engine that flies Africa to greater heights by continuously improving our Global Supply Chain Management using international data analysis to provide suitable high quality construction and building material solutions, whilst imparting employees and PG Service Providers with product and technical skills-training and development opportunities.

02. Mission

We are the one stop construction and building materials solution centre in Zimbabwe and the region and create value in building dream homes and commercial infrastructure in an easy way. We :-

1. Offer competitive pricing through Global Supply Chain Management

2. Work efficiently by adopting the latest technology and systems

3. Use international data analysis to provide suitable product solutions

4. Offer skills training for employees and PG service providers

5. Increase our footprint by opening factories in Africa that produce locally

03. Values

Trust – we maintain the highest levels of professionalism, integrity, honesty and fairness in our relationships with our customers, stakeholders, staff and the general public.

Win–Win Strategy - we adopt strategies that mutually benefit the organization, the supplier and customer

Employee development - we provide an environment that fosters professional growth and personal development.

Customer experience – we make decisions based on how well it serves our customers.

Constant improvement – we intensely focus on how we can operate more efficiently.

Innovation – we challenge our ideas of what is possible in order to better meet the needs of our customers

PG Steel

PG Steel profiles and distributes roofing steel sheets and ridges (chromadek and galvanised) from imported steel coils. The sheets come in different profiles mainly corrugated and IBR (Invented Box Rib) and are profiled to customer’s specific sizes and colours.

Products : Corrugated iron sheets , IBR sheets and chromadek , ridges , valley gutters

Services: Bullnose (rounding/shaping of an edge)

PG Center

PG Centre is Zimbabwe’s One-stop building solution centre offering a new life style shopping experience and selection from a wide variety of quality products. The mega building solution store offers a consistent buying experience (PG New Lifestyle) in all the PG Centre outlets countrywide by incorporating a diverse set of businesses (PG Timbers, PG Glass, PG Steel, PG Paints and Zimtile) around a single PG brand. The unit has branches in major towns and cities across the country, viz:

Harare   Bindura
Bulawayo   Gweru
Mutare   Chinhoyi
Masvingo   Kwekwe

Products: Structural Building Materials, Plumbing & Electrical, Painting, Glass & Aluminium, Kitchen materials & appliances, Ceiling, Safety clothing & equipment, Tools & hardware, Wall & Floor finishes, Lighting & Electrical, Sanitary ware, window and stairs

PG Timbers

PG Timbers specialises in the manufacturing and distribution of a wide range of timber products, specializing in the manufacturing of pre-fabricated trusses using the Mitek 2020 Software Engineered Design.  Its core business is applying timber and boards into finished products which include kitchen cupboards & BICs and doors that are used in all forms of construction, furniture industry and home.

PG Glass

PG Glass is the distributor of building float glass, architectural and safety glass.   This business unit focuses on providing high quality glass and boasts of specialist skills in glazing.   The division also manufactures Masterglaze putty at its plant in Harare

Products: Building Glass, Laminates, Mirrors, Putty

Services: Glass cutting to size, Polishing, Drilling, Bevelling, Glazing and on-site estimates with unparalleled technical back-up service.
